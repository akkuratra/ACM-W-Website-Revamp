About Us

To Celebrate Women in Computing

The UPES ACM-W was found in April, 2015 with an aim to realize the dynamic image of women in the field of computers and information technology and to provide them with a platform to showcase their technical skills. We at UPES ACM-W try to follow in their steps to create a community where all are given equal opportunities and everyone works together in harmony. UPES ACM-W Student Chapter is reserved exclusively for women with an aim to highlight the fact that women are as competent as men in the field of technology. To achieve this, we organize various activities and events to bring out the programmer and genius within each and every individual. We strive to create a healthy environment for women that encourages them to step forward and showcase their talent.

ACM-W believes that putting women's vision into effect would give way to a brighter future.

Initiatives

UPES ACM is a student chapter created to address technical needs of the students pursuing computers in UPES. The chapter works toward broadening the horizons of the students related to their course, but apart from their coursework. To work towards this goal,the chapter organises various events including workshops and mentorship programs with people related to computers and well-verse with their areas of interest.

01

SPY-C

A way for students to learn C and interact with their seniors. UPES ACM devised this initiative to help introduce students to programming languages and their delights. Spy C melds the rules of code with the imagination of humans.

02

WORKSHOPS

Recognising the importance of nurturing the various interests of our members, UPES-ACM and ACM-W hosts interactive technical workshops that helps foster innovation and sharpen skills. We at UPES ACM and ACM W, think of ways to make the world a better place by being the change we want to see.

03

CODE ANYTIME

Code anytime, an initiative augmented by UPES ACM and ACM-W in an effort to develop a coding culture within campus. An environment where everyone is provided with the opportunity to code, learn and grow.
